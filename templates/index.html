About OpenGameMusic

OpenGameMusic is a platform where musicians can share their music and game developers can find tracks to use in their projects. Our goal is to connect creators and provide high-quality, royalty-free music for games.

Features

  • Upload and share music with the community.
  • Tag tracks by genre, mood, and usage type.
  • Discover music for your game using our search and filter tools.
  • Collaborate with musicians and developers.
